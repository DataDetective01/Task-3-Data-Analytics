 Project 3: SQL Data Analysis 
 Project Overview
 This project is completed as part of the Decodelabs Data Analytics Internship Program
 The goal is to master the fundamental art of writing structured queries to:
filter, group, and aggregate raw data into actionable business intelligence.
Tools used:
DB Browser for SQlite
VS Code
Dataset Description
The sales_dataset with 1200 records and 14 fields including OrderID, Date, Product, Quantity, UnitPrice, TotalPrice, PaymentMethod, OrderStatus, ReferralSource.
SQL Concepts Covered
-SELECT
-WHERE
-ORDER BY
-GROUP BY
-COUNT()
-SUM()
-AVG()
-HAVING
Business Questions Answered
-Select all orders of 1200 records.
-Filter delivered 231 orders.
-Find orders with Totalprice greater than 2000.
-Sort orders by TotalPrice from highest to lowest, which tablet topped the orders.
-Count total orders by product grouped into 7 products.
-Calculate average order value by product and chair product recorded the highest revenue.
-Analyze orders and revenue by referral source: Instagram leads.
-Analyze orders and revenue by payment method and Online dominates.
-Identify products generating revenue a specified threshold.
Key Insights
-The chair product generated the highest total revenue.
-Instagram is the top referral source with approximately 1.26M revenue 
-Online is the most payment method.
-250 orders were cancelled over. 20% cancellation rate.
-38 premium orders were both delivered and above 2000.
